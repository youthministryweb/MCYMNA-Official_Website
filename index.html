WHO WE ARE ...

We are the Malankara Catholic Youth Movement of North America! We strive to promote within our members the growth of Catholic spiritualism, faith, and values, as well as an understanding of all facets of the Malankara Catholic Church. We promote fellowship and the development of leadership skills and multitude of talents amongst its members with the love and power of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.
