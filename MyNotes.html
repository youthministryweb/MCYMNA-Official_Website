We are thrilled to announce the upcoming MCYM North American Youth Conference, a vibrant celebration of faith and community set to unfold in the heart of Austin, Texas , at the awe-inspiring St. Edwards University!

Mark your calendars from August 1st, 2024 - August 4th, 2024 , as we gear up for an extraordinary conference filled with faith-filled energy and the joyous camaraderie of like-minded souls.

From a heart-pounding concert featuring a famous Catholic rapper to thrilling games and dances designed to foster lasting friendships. There's something for everyone! Also, get ready to engage in immersive spiritual sessions and discussions, thoughtfully crafted to nourish your soul and deepen your connection to the Malankara Catholic faith.
